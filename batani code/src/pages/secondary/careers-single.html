Your role at Batani LLC

We are looking for developers who are really passionate about coding, and who are familiar with public blockchains. We use Algorand to power our platform, so any previous experience with Algorand will be helpful.

We want developers who are interested in theory. So if your typical schedule is to eat, sleep and code, and you intravenously refuel yourself with latte's then we have a place for you at Batani!

Responsibilities

  • Server Side Application
  • Brainstorming, and applying new innovative features
  • Supervising back-end development team
  • Collaborating with department leaders
  • Implementing best practices, and staying abreast of industry trends.

Requirements

  • Experience with Algorand or other public blockchains.
  • 5-6 years of back-end experience in Python or Golang.
  • 1-2 years of experience with HTML, CSS, and JS.
  • Problem Solving Skills.
  • Passionate about coding.

Oue values

Team work

Building an application of this size, with this complexity, requires a variety of skill-sets. We value hard work, intelligence, and creativity, but we also value people who are willing and able to work with others.

Entrepreneurial Spirit

It is not easy to disrupt a multi billion dollar industry. Working at Batani is NOT a 9 to 5 job. If you have a desire to be on the front-lines of technology, and want to be a part of something special, then we'd love to hear from you.
