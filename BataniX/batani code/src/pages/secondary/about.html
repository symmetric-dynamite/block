Our vision

From searching properties, to scheduling appointments, to signing contracts, we aim to reduce complexity and increase effiency, while simultaneously increasing transparency and security.

We are a small team of entreprenuers who want to change the way people transact. With the advent of blockchain technology, and the immutability of smart contracts, we no longer need the traditional real estate company.

When real estate companies markup the price, renters and buyers always end up paying more, and sellers often get less. By removing the middle-men we can capture additional value for both buyers and sellers.
